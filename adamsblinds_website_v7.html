About Adams Blinds

London’s independent blind specialist since 2009

Adams Blinds was founded in London to do one thing well: supply and fit made-to-measure window treatments for homes and businesses across the city. We are independent. We are not part of a franchise. Every job is handled by our own team, from the first conversation to the final fitting.
