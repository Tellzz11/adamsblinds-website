About Adams Blinds

London-made, London-minded

"We built this business on the principle that your windows deserve the same attention as every other design decision in your home."

Adams Blinds was founded in 2009 by a family who believed London's homes deserved better than the generic, mass-produced window dressings filling high street showrooms. Fifteen years and 4,000 installations later, that belief hasn't changed — but our team, our fabric library and our range certainly have.

Every member of our team — from the advisors who visit your home to the fitters who install your blinds — is employed by us directly. We never subcontract. We never rush. We never compromise.

FAQ

Questions we get asked

Prices vary by product and fabric, but as a guide: roller blinds start from £85 fitted, venetian blinds from £110, roman blinds from £120, plantation shutters from £350 per panel, and curtains from £195 per pair. We provide a fixed price quote during your free home consultation with no hidden extras.

We do both, but the vast majority of our customers choose our full supply and professional fitting service. Our prices include expert fitting by our own trained installers — never subcontracted. We'll also take care of disposing of any old blinds or curtains.

Typically 7–14 working days from the point you approve your order. We'll confirm lead times based on your chosen products when we visit. Our roller blinds and standard venetians can often be fitted faster — we'll always be transparent about timelines upfront.

We cover all of Greater London including Central, North, South, East and West London. Our most frequent areas include Kensington, Chelsea, Notting Hill, Islington, Clapham, Wimbledon, Richmond and Canary Wharf — but if you're in the M25 corridor, just ask and we'll likely be able to help.

All our products come with a 5-year manufacturer's guarantee covering material and mechanism defects. Our fitting work is additionally guaranteed for 2 years. In the unlikely event of any issue, we'll visit your home to assess and resolve it at no charge within this period.
