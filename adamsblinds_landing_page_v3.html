Getting the Right Blinds Shouldn't Be This Hard

You've measured twice. You've ordered online. The parcel arrives and — they don't fit. Or the colour is nothing like the screen. Or the fitting instructions might as well be in another language. For thousands of London homeowners, buying window coverings has become an exercise in frustration, wasted weekends and money thrown away on returns.

Even when you find a local fitter, the uncertainty doesn't stop. Will they measure accurately? Will they turn up when they say they will? Will the finished result look like the showroom photo — or something considerably less impressive? These are real concerns, and they're the reason so many people put the project off entirely, living with old blinds or bare windows far longer than they should.

There is a better way. One where a qualified expert comes to your home, takes every measurement precisely, guides you through every option, and fits everything to a professional standard — with a guarantee that covers you if anything isn't right. That's exactly what Adams Blinds has been delivering across London for over a decade.

Our Process

From First Call to Perfect Fit

Our entire process is designed to take the work off your hands and put the results you want on your windows.

1

We Visit Your Home

One of our expert consultants comes to you at a time that suits your schedule. We bring a full range of fabric and material samples so you can see exactly how every option looks in your own light, against your own walls.

2

We Measure & Quote

We take precise, professional measurements of every window — completely free of charge, with no obligation. You'll receive a clear, itemised quote on the spot. If you've had another written quote, we'll match or beat it.

3

We Fit Everything Perfectly

When your blinds, shutters or curtains are ready, we return to fit them to an exacting standard. Every installation comes with our 6-month fitting warranty — if anything isn't right, we fix it at no cost to you.
