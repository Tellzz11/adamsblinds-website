## Product Catalogue

### 1. Plantation Shutters
- Premium product, highest margin
- Price: From £350 per panel fitted
- Materials: ABS polymer (moisture resistant), real hardwood, MDF
- Styles: Full height, café style, tier-on-tier, solid panel, tracked (for bay/bifold)
- Louvre sizes: 47mm, 63mm, 89mm
- Colours: Brilliant white, chalk white, grey, bespoke paint match
- Key features: Light control, insulation, adds value to property, low maintenance
- Best for: Victorian terraces, period properties, bay windows

### 2. Roller Blinds
- Most popular entry-level product
- Price: From £85 fitted
- Fabrics: Blockout, solar screen, sheer, printed
- Features: Motorisation available, child-safe chain options
- Key use: Kitchens, offices, modern apartments

### 3. Roman Blinds
- Premium fabric option
- Price: From £120 fitted
- Fabrics: Linen, velvet, cotton, patterned
- Features: Soft structured folds, custom lining options (thermal, blackout)
- Best for: Living rooms, bedrooms, period homes

### 4. Venetian Blinds
- Price: From £110 fitted
- Materials: Aluminium (25mm, 50mm), real wood (25mm, 50mm), faux wood
- Features: Infinitely adjustable light control, moisture resistant options
- Best for: Home offices, kitchens, contemporary spaces

### 5. Curtains & Drapes
- Price: From £195 per pair fitted
- Fabrics: Linen, velvet, chenille, silk look, cotton
- Header styles: Eyelet, pencil pleat, pinch pleat, wave
- Lining: Unlined, standard, blackout, thermal, interlining
- Best for: Living rooms, bedrooms, Georgian/Victorian homes

### 6. Motorised / Smart Blinds
- Price: From £220 fitted (per blind)
- Control: App (iOS/Android), remote control, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit
- Products: Motorised rollers, motorised romans, motorised venetians, motorised shutters
- Best for: Skylights, high windows, smart home setups

### 7. Vertical Blinds
- Price: From £75 fitted
- Materials: Fabric, PVC
- Features: Wide width coverage, patio/sliding door coverage
- Best for: Offices, conservatories, wide windows, patio doors

### 8. Day/Night (Vision) Blinds
- Price: From £95 fitted
- Features: Alternating sheer and opaque fabric bands, privacy with light without blackout
- Also called: Zebra blinds, vision blinds, duo blinds
- Best for: Living rooms, offices needing privacy and light

### 9. Perfect Fit / Pleated Blinds
- Price: From £85 fitted
- Features: Clips directly into uPVC window frame (no drilling required), perfect for tilt-and-turn windows
- Types: Pleated, cellular/honeycomb (best insulation), blackout options
- Best for: uPVC windows, conservatories, bathrooms

### 10. Outdoor Blinds / Awnings
- Price: From £450 fitted
- Types: Retractable awnings, drop arm awnings, exterior roller blinds
- Features: UV protection, wind sensor option, motorised option
- Best for: Patios, garden rooms, conservatories (exterior shade)

### Commercial / Contract Range
- Office blinds (roller, venetian, vertical, motorised)
- Hospitality (hotels, restaurants — custom FR fabrics)
- Healthcare (anti-microbial fabrics, easy clean)
- Education
